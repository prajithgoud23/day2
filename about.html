The Range Rover is a luxury SUV made by Land Rover, a British car company owned by Tata Motors. It is famous for its premium design, powerful engine, and high comfort. Range Rover cars are used for both city driving and off-road travel because they have strong performance and advanced technology. The interior is very luxurious with leather seats, modern touch screens, and safety features. Due to its high quality and style, the Range Rover is one of the most expensive and prestigious cars in the world.
